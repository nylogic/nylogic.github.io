--- layout: talk title: Model Theory Seminar talk_title: First-order sentences in random groups categories: model-theory-seminar date: 2025-03-07 semester: spring-2025 speaker_first: Olga speaker_last: Kharlampovich speaker_website: "https://hunter.cuny.edu/people/olga-kharlampovich/" affiliation: CUNY abstract: "

We prove that a random group, in Gromov's density model with $d\lt 1/2$, satisfies an AE sentence (in the language of groups) if and only if this sentence is true in a nonabelian free group. This is a joint work with R. Sklinos.
