--- layout: talk title: Model Theory Seminar talk_title: Monadic stability and growth rates of omega-categorical structures categories: model-theory-seminar date: 2019-09-27 semester: fall-2019 speaker_first: Sam speaker_last: Braunfeld speaker_website: "http://math.umd.edu/~sbraunf/" affiliation: University of Maryland abstract: "Generalizing the classical combinatorial problem of counting the orbits of a group acting on a finite set, we consider the growth rate of an omega-categorical structure M, which counts the orbits of Aut(M) acting on n-sets. We show that for stable M, there is a gap from subexponential to superexponential growth, corresponding to whether M is monadically stable. This allows us to confirm some longstanding conjectures of Macpherson about the spectrum of possible growth rates." excerpt_separator: talk: yes note: ---
