Much work on elementary submodels of recursively saturated models of PA was done, beginning in the 1980s, by Craig Smory≈Ñski, Richard Kaye, Henryk Kotlarski, Jim Schmerl, and myself. The set of all elementary substructures of a recursively saturated model $M$ ordered by inclusion forms a lattice $Lt(M)$. Kotlarski asked whether $Lt(M)$ depends on $M$. In the talk, I will describe the architecture of $Lt(M)$, and I will survey what is known and what is still open about Kotlarski's question.
