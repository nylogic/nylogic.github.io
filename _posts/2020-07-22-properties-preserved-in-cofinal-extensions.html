--- layout: talk title: MOPA talk_title: Properties preserved in cofinal extensions categories: MOPA date: 2020-07-22 semester: spring-2020 speaker_first: Tin Lok speaker_last: Wong speaker_website: "https://blog.nus.edu.sg/matwong/" affiliation: National University of Singapore abstract: " Cofinal extensions generally preserve many more properties of a model of arithmetic than their sisters, end extensions. Exactly how much must or can they preserve? The answer is intimately related to how much arithmetic the model can do. I will survey what is known and what is not known about this question, and report on some recent work on this line.
