--- layout: talk title: Set Theory Seminar talk_title: Compactness and minimality properties for linear orderings categories: set-theory-seminar date: 2026-05-08 semester: spring-2026 speaker_first: James speaker_last: Cummings speaker_website: "https://www.cmu.edu/math/people/faculty/cummings.html" affiliation: Carnegie Mellon University abstract: "

Let $\Phi$ be a property of linear orderings such that if $L$ has $\Phi$ and $L$ is bi-embeddable with $L'$ then $L'$ has $\Phi$.
We discuss some situations where:
A) (Incompactness) $L$ does not have $\Phi$ but every strictly smaller suborder of $L$ has $\Phi$.
B) (Minimality) $L$ does not have $\Phi$, and $L$ embeds into every subordering of $L$ which does not have $\Phi$.
